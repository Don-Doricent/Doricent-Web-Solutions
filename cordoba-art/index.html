Hi! I'm Frankie Cordoba

Welcome to my website! I'm Frankie Cordoba, an abstract artist who creates visually stunning pieces of art. My passion for exploring color, form, and texture is reflected in my artwork, which is a unique and intricate blend of organic and geometric shapes. I strive to create pieces that evoke a sense of energy and movement, inviting the viewer to engage with the piece and explore its many layers. I hope you enjoy exploring my art as much as I enjoy creating it!
